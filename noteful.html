The Original Challenge

For my senior HCI capstone project, my team worked with Noteful, a e-learning startup that teaches music theory.

When we first got onboarded to this project, our client originally requested for a revamp of their beta student dashboard to get ready for their offical launch, working mostly on interface improvements that would appeal to their audience of young kids.

Research Insights

Music theory learning comes off as monotonous and unengaging.

Students learn music theory in conjunction to something broader, like playing the piano, but unlike that, music theory comes off as monotonous. Meanwhile, traditional worksheets and music theory apps aren't suitable for young learners, and what they want is to learn alongside friends and have fun while they're at it.

New Approach

How might we design an engaging learning experience for young music theory learners to create long-term motivation?

After presenting our rationale with Noteful, we aligned everyone on the team and shifted our focus from dashboard design to better meet user needs, updating our problem statement accordingly.

Path View
