The Original Challenge

Design a new dashboard with engaging features that allow students to track their learning progress.

For my senior HCI capstone project, my team worked with Noteful, a e-learning startup that teaches music theory.

When we first got onboarded to this project, our client originally requested for a revamp of their beta student dashboard to get ready for their offical launch, working mostly on interface improvements that would appeal to their audience of young kids.
