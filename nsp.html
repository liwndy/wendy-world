Graduate Assistant Project, Spring 2025

The Norman Sicily Project

The Norman Sicily Project, a NEH-funded project, documents the island’s cultural heritage. Their website brings together an extensive collection of maps, photos, introductory essays, data, and more, making it both a research tool and an educational resource. Partnering with another designer, I was tasked with revamping the website to bring clarity and usability to this wealth of information, ensuring that diverse audiences can easily explore Sicily’s Norman past.
