Welcome :) I am Wendy Li, a product designer and Master's student studying design @ Pratt, currently based in NYC.

Curiosity has always been at the heart of my craft. Back in middle school, it meant spending countless hours tinkering in Photoshop CS5 just to see what I could create. That same sense of wonder later guided me through college, where I explored diverse fields, from business, psychology, to computer science, before discovering my true passion in human-computer interaction. Each detour added new layers to how I think and create, shaping me into the designer I am today.

I am someone who thrives on learning, connecting ideas across disciplines, and pushing the boundaries of what design can make possible for communities. Now, as I finish my master’s degree, I’m continuing to deepen both my technical and creative practice. To me, design is more than problem-solving: it’s a way to stay endlessly curious about people, technology, and the world around us, and I excited to see where that curiosity leads me next in my journey as a product designer!
