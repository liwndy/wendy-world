Hackathon Project, Spring 2025

Amazon Music's Song of the Day

In today’s competitive streaming landscape, music consumption has become highly personalized but often isolated. Despite music being inherently social, most platforms offer limited ways for listeners to connect meaningfully through it. This project reimagines how Amazon Music can foster daily engagement and emotional connection through Song of the Day, a lightweight and shared experience.
